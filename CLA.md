## How to Sign

You do **not** need to sign a physical document. The CLA is accepted electronically:

1. Open a Pull Request against the Project repository.
2. The **CLA Assistant** bot will comment on your PR asking you to sign.
3. Click the link and follow the instructions to sign with your GitHub account.
4. Once signed, the bot will mark your PR as CLA-compliant.

Your signature is recorded in the repository's `.clabot` configuration and is valid for all future contributions.
